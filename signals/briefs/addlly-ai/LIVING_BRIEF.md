## Thesis
Addlly AI is a Singapore-based GenAI content-marketing startup building a zero-prompt enterprise AI content suite with multi-language Asian-market support. Its nomination to the SuperAI Genesis Top 50 cohort in mid-2026 signals growing traction for its Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) platform among enterprise customers including Johnson's Baby, MetLife, and Shiseido.
