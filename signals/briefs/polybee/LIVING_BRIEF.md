## Thesis
Polybee is an NUS GRIP-incubated Singapore agritech startup deploying physical AI agents for precision pollination and yield forecasting in specialty crops. Having raised a $4.3M seed round in early 2026, the company has expanded to Australia with an ANZ headquarters in Victoria, partnering with La Trobe University, Hort Innovation, and major growers. Founder Siddharth Jadhav projects A$10M revenue within two years from 5 key crops including leafy greens and berries.
