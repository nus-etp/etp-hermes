# Carousell — LIVING BRIEF
_Last updated: 2026-05-15 15:31 UTC_

## Thesis
Carousell is a Singapore-headquartered online classifieds and consumer marketplace platform that also operates OneShift, Mudah, and Cho Tot across Southeast Asia. The platform continues to emphasize trust and safety as a competitive differentiator, with recent data showing over 99% of transactions are scam-free even as fraud tactics evolve and become more sophisticated.

## Profile
- Sector: Consumer marketplace / online classifieds
- Region: Southeast Asia (Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Vietnam)
