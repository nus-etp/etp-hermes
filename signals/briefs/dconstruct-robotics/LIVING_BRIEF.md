## Thesis
dConstruct Robotics is a Singapore-headquartered deep-tech startup building SLAM-based autonomous navigation and 3D digital-twin systems for construction, defense, and logistics. Founded in 2021 by Chinn Hwa Lim, the company has developed the d.ASH platform (d.ASH Nav, d.ASH Xplorer, d.ASH Ops) and signed a manufacturing MOU with Foxconn. In June 2026 it appointed He Ruijie (PhD, MIT), a two-time robotics startup co-founder, to its Tech Council as an advisor, signaling an intensifying focus on engineering talent and product direction.
